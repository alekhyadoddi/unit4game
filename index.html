Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above your random number.The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time when the game starts , the game will change the values of each crystal
